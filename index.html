Content Automation Project

This is a personal-use application for managing and automating content creation and posting to social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest) that I own and operate. The application generates short-form video content and schedules it to my own accounts, and reads basic analytics/trend data to inform future content.

This is a single-user, personal project. It does not serve, manage, or access accounts belonging to any other individuals or businesses.
