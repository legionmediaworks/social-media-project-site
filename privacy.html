Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how this personal content automation application ("the App") accesses and handles data from connected social media accounts (Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest).

Information We Access

The App accesses data only from social media accounts that I personally own and administer. This may include account profile information, posted content metadata, engagement metrics (likes, views, comments, shares), and trend/keyword data made available through each platform's official API.

How Information Is Used

Data accessed through these APIs is used solely to generate, schedule, and post content to my own accounts, and to analyze the performance of that content for personal reference. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to any third party.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on infrastructure I personally own and control. It is not hosted on shared or third-party servers.

Single-User Application

This application is for personal use only and does not authenticate, manage, or access accounts belonging to any other individuals, businesses, or organizations.

Your Rights

Access granted to this application can be revoked at any time through the security or app-permissions settings of each respective platform (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest).
